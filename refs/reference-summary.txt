One Meta Observation (likely useful for your framing)

Across fields, three distinct strategies emerge for making causal claims about the future:

Structural invariance (Lucas, MTE, SCMs)

Statistical transport / reweighting

Explicit modeling of heterogeneity or dynamics

Your proposed future-looking estimands sit very naturally at the intersection of these traditions — which is a strong positioning opportunity.
